Name of Study


Purpose of Study

To examine the metabolic responses of recumbent cycling exercise


Lab

Queen's Muscle Physiology Laboratory

Who's Doing the Study

Nick Preobrzenski

Location

Kingston, ON

Compensation Info

$150 will be provided to those who complete the study


Benefits of Participating in Study

1) Aerobic Fitness Testing (e.g. VO2peak) that is usually inaccessible to the general public (i.e. you'll get to see your level of fitness)
2) Guided exercise sessions





Inclusion/Exclusion Criteria

Inclusion: (1) Male, less than 3 hours of physical activity per week, no previous cycling training, no concurrent involvement in another training program, BMI < 30 kg/m2
Exclusion: (1) Presence of cardiovascular disease, presence of metabolic disease, taking regular oral medication, current smoker









Participant Expectations

Participants will be expected to visit the laboratory on 4 separate occasions (excluding screening). The first 2 will be for fitness testing, and the next 2 will be for exercising on a recumbent bike. Before each of the 2 exercise sessions on a recumbent bike, a muscle biopsy will be taken. After each of 2 exercise sessions, biopsies will be taken immediately after and 3 hours after finishing the exercise (total number of biopsies = 6)
